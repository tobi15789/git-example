The BEST Apple Pie Recipe
Pie crust
Apples
Butter
Flour
Cinnamon
Sugar
Egg Wash


cooking steps
1. **Preheat oven** to 400°F (200°C).

2. **Prepare the apples**  
    - Peel, core, and slice the apples.  
    - Toss with sugar, cinnamon, and a little flour.

3. **Make the crust**  
    - Roll out half the pie crust and place it in a pie dish.  
    - Trim the edges, leaving a little overhang.

4. **Add filling**  
    - Spread the apple mixture evenly into the crust.  
    - Dot the top with small pieces of butter.

5. **Top crust**  
    - Roll out the second crust and place it over the apples.  
    - Seal and crimp the edges.  
    - Cut a few small slits in the top for steam.

6. **Egg wash**  
    - Brush the top crust with beaten egg wash.

7. **Bake**  
    - Bake for 45–55 minutes, until golden brown and bubbling.

8. **Cool and serve**  
    - Let the pie cool for at least 1 hour before slicing.
